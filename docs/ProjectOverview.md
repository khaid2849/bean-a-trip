## Project Vision

BeanATrip is an all-in-one travel planning and trip management platform that centralizes all travel-related information in a single workspace.

Instead of using multiple tools such as Google Sheets, Google Maps, Notes, Messenger, Zalo, Email, and Calendar, users can manage everything inside one application.

The application should support the complete travel lifecycle:

1. Before the trip

   * Research destinations
   * Create itineraries
   * Estimate budgets
   * Prepare checklists
   * Manage bookings

2. During the trip

   * Follow daily schedules
   * Track expenses
   * Record actual spending
   * Access booking information
   * Update plans on the go

3. After the trip

   * Review expenses
   * Store memories and photos
   * Write travel journals
   * Analyze trip statistics

## Target Users

### Primary Users

* Couples traveling together
* Self-planned travelers

### Future Users

* Friend groups
* Families
* Travel communities

## Core Problems

Travelers currently face several problems:

* Travel information is scattered across multiple applications.
* Budget tracking is difficult during trips.
* Booking information is hard to organize.
* Planning and actual execution often differ.
* Expense sharing is inconvenient.
* Travel memories are not well organized after the trip.
